What is Liferadar?

Life is multidimensional. Work, health, relationships, faith, finances — each area deserves conscious attention.

Liferadar gives you a structured, visual framework to define the pillars that matter most to you, score them regularly, and turn your reflections into concrete decisions.

Whether you're an individual striving for balance or a family admin coordinating growth across your household, Liferadar adapts to your needs.
